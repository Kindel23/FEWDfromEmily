Fun Whale Shark facts

Did you know?


Whale shark swimming and feeding
Largest fish
Whale Sharks are the biggest fish in the sea. They can grow up to 40 feet long.
Filter feeder
Whale Sharks scoop 1000's of gallons of water through their mouths and filter out tiny plankton which sustains them. Thjey have no teeth!
They are endangered
The species is considered endangered due to shark finning, fishing by-catch and vessel collisions.
Reproduction
Whale Sharks don't reach sexual maturity until 30 years. They give live birth.
Identify Whale Sharks
Every whale shark has a unique pattern of spots, much like a thumbprint. This is used to id whale sharks. You can help study whale sharks by uploading images so scientists can track the whale sharks. For more information visit: whaleshark.org
